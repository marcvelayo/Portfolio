Project 2: Hello, My Name Is Anxiety

Hello, My Name Is Anxiety, Digital Video, 3:07min, Vimeo

Mental health is one of the things that our society needs to prioritize. We encounter various individuals everyday, and we can never tell what's going on with their lives.

Emotions are hard to express especially if one's vulnerability is on the line. This video will be tackling the stigma surrounding mental health, mainly anxiety.

It features Mika Manalastas, a close friend who's going through this mental disorder. How does she cope with it? How does she express it?

This video interacts with reality and technology. Social Media is one of the platforms that our society uses today for expression. It's a helpful tool but to what extent?
