Project 4: I am the Cyberbully

I am the Cyberbully, Digital Video, 2:06min, Vimeo.

Posting photos and videos are some of the amazing features that social media provides us.

But sometimes, this feature gets abused-- through the different content posted online. In the video, the girl finds out about the circulating photos of her online.

These circulating photos was originally taken by someone during the incident. It was then posted online-- where people started sharing and sending it.

The Internet is one of the biggest platforms in the world-- reaching the whole world. With all these content posted, how is she gonna restart her life?

The video tackles the emotional damage that she's going through. Will it ever be okay?
