Project 1: Look Up

Look Up, Digital Video, 2:01min, Youtube.

Technology has taken over our lives--- you should be scared.

We all spend a lot of time on our phones, looking down and not realizing the amount of time wasted on social media, games or other things that our phones provide to us

We forget to look up to the reality of life-- the things that are happening around us

We need to choose life, so please--- choose to LOOK UP.
